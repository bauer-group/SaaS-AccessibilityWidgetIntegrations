# Contributing & Licensing of Contributions

<a id="english"></a>

**🇬🇧 English** · [🇩🇪 Deutsch](#-deutsch)

This repository contains the **integrations** for the BAUER GROUP Accessibility
Widget. They are permissively licensed (MIT; the Drupal module GPL-2.0-or-later).
Because there is **no dual-licensing** here, no copyright-assignment or relicensing
CLA is required — contributions follow the standard **inbound = outbound** model.

## Inbound = Outbound

By submitting a contribution (a pull request, patch, or any other code,
documentation, or material), you agree that your contribution is licensed under
the **same license as the file(s) you modify**:

- **MIT License** for everything in this repository, **except**
- **GNU GPL-2.0-or-later** for the Drupal module (`packages/cms/drupal/...`), as
  required by Drupal.org.

You retain copyright to your contribution; you simply license it under the terms
above so the project can use, ship, and sublicense it.

> Note: This applies only to the integrations in **this** repository. The
> Accessibility Widget itself lives in a separate, dual-licensed repository with
> its own contribution terms.

## Developer Certificate of Origin

Please sign off your commits (`git commit -s`) to certify the
[Developer Certificate of Origin 1.1](https://developercertificate.org/): that you
wrote the contribution or have the right to submit it under the license above.

```text
Signed-off-by: Your Name <you@example.com>
```

Du behältst das Urheberrecht an deinem Beitrag; du lizenzierst ihn lediglich unter
den obigen Bedingungen, damit das Projekt ihn nutzen, ausliefern und sublizenzieren
kann.
